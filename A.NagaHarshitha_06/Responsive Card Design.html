Demon SlayerKimetsu no Yaiba is a Japanese anime series about a young boy who becomes a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister who has turned into a demon.The story takes place in early 20th century Japan, where man-eating demons roam the land. Tanjiro Kamado's family is slaughtered, but his sister Nezuko survives as a demon. Tanjiro trains to join the Demon Slayer Corps, an underground organization of skilled fighters who protect humans from demons.
