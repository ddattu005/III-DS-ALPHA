Inception (2010)

About Inception

Director: Christopher Nolan

Genre: Science Fiction, Action, Thriller

Release Date: July 16, 2010

A thief who steals corporate secrets through dream-sharing technology is given the inverse task of planting an idea into the mind of a CEO. However, his tragic past makes the mission far more challenging than expected.

Starring: Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, Marion Cotillard, Ken Watanabe, Cillian Murphy, Michael Caine

Box Office: Over $836 million worldwide

Awards: Academy Awards for Best Cinematography, Best Sound Editing, Best Sound Mixing, and Best Visual Effects
